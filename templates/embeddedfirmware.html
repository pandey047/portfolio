    IT ESP-12F Programming

    I’ve been designing around the “ESP-12E” or “ESP-12F”, which has 4MB of flash, an onboard antenna, and the majority of the GPIO pins available around the edge. The signature of these modules is the tin RF shield with the “AI” stamp on the front. The main difference between the “E” and “F” is that third row of pins at the bottom which, actually, you’re never going to use. Also, the shape of the antenna differs slightly.
